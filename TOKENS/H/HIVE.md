---

Hive is a blockchain ecosystem designed for the easy storage and retrieval of information. Its fast and free transactions (3 sec. block times & transactions confirmed within milliseconds) make it well suited for content creators, investors and developers to share data and earn crypto through the interactive applications built on its platform. 

$HIVE is the native token of the network used to upvote content and power its ecosystem of decentralized applications (dApps).
