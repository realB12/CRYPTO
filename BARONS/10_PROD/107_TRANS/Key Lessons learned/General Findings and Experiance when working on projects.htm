Presentation = 2 Presenters

Presentations and Kick Off Meetings should be hold by 2 people: so the other one has time to rethink and taking notes where the other one keeps up the speed. The other can assist and express his view when somethins is difficult to explain.

Delegate List of needs

At the Kick Off meeging in Athens (George) it turned out a success that when you install yourself at a new location that you sit down, define a list of all things you need and hand it out for a responsible person. (Preparation List Kick Off: busSD102.htm).
