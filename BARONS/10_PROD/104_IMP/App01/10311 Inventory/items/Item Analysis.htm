Guideline Notes

  1. The following  topics should be part of every Vendor Item Analysis Report which will be audited for all vendor items with Business Risk (BR) = 4 or 5.
  2. Further note that the guideline is used to ensure thoroughness and consistency in the assessment. It does not indicate what constitutes compliance.
  3. Everything in italic is to explain the various topics for this generic template. The rest, in non italic, is what you might use as headings for your report.

Contents of Report

This report will cover only assessment issues of the item itself. The vendor's internal compliance will be covered by a separate document. Also note here that detailed Documentation for the item's (test) planning, conversion and validation is part of other reports.

Purpose

Purpose of this report is  to summarize all Y2K relevant information, to link this items to business activities, to provide a basis for risk rating and to reduce response time in case of problems.

General Questions

  1. Does the vendor provide access to the source code ?
  2. Does the vendor hand out assessment documents ?
  3. Does the vendor provide test plans ?
  4. Have you access to the involved data in supporting databases.
    When yes:
    • have you analyzed the data according to the database analysis checklist ?
      What was the outcome ?
  5. What are the programming language used for development ?
  6. Is the item  relying on products from other 3rd party providers ? When yes: which are they and how is their compliance status ?

When status of the item is unknown

  1. Is the product compliant according to the KUONI Standard Compliance Definition document ?
  2. see bucsd100.htm in the kick off package
  3. Which compliance status has the item reached to date (date and status) for a list of all compliance status see the document complian.htm in the kick off package.
  4. Does the item currently in use at KUONI directly import or export date-related data to any other application, equipment and/or system within the vendors family of products ?
  5. When yes: which are they and what is their compliance level
  6. Does the item use third party vendor software ? If yes, please provide the name and release of the software and indicate its Year2000 compliance status.
  7. Does the item use/access a third party vendor database. If yes, please provide the name and release of the software and indicate if it is Year2000 compliant.
