Immediate Activity List

Draft Version 1.0, December 05, 1997 bucSD076.htm

 

The following topics may require immediate actions. Delay may put your organisation at risk !

We cannot stress enough how important it is that you create a Year2000 project that is staffed with adequate resources to position your organisation for this transition. If you have not yet begun to size the effort and position yourselves, we strongly recommend that you begin as soon as possible.

May be we cannot address certification streamline according to the methodology. So that's why we have listed here some key tasks you may start already now.

think about the following resources you need
  • Your local Y2K Project Manager (OrgMan) with at least 80% of its time till at least assessment is completed
  • Provide a Y2K test environment (71) where you can test your key applications with the system clock in the next millennium (warping)
  • Think about aquisition of testing tools
  • Evaluate Test Planning capacity
For all your 3rd party products: For all products which are key and unique to your organisation and therefore will not be covered by the Programme Office for Vendor Management contact your vendor and ask him about compliance and a strategy to build a Y2K test environment to prove it.
Make sure you have at least Year2000-ready (system) software installed, including the applicable service.
Make usre you have enough licences for all your software
