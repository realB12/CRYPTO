Macadamia is a native iOS client for **[Cashu](../C/Cashu.md)**, the Chaumian eCash system designed for near perfect privacy.

Macadamia support standard cashu operations such as:

* Minting of tokens
* Sending and
* Receiving
* Melting tokens (using them to pay a Lightning Network invoice)
* Restoring your wallet balance using a 12 word mnemonic seed phrase backup
