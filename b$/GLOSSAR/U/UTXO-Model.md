# DAS UTXO Modell
Das UTXO Modell wurde unabhängig von Bitcoin entwickelt und wird lediglich - wie von anderen Cryptos auch - von Bitcoin genutzt. 

Because cryptocurrencies were created with anonymity in mind, UTXOs are associated with the public addresses visible to the entire network. Users cannot be identified from their ownership—unless they advertise their address—but the model allows for transparency through the addresses.
