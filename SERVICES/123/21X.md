# 21X.com
(a tokenisation company)

* [Webseite](https://21x.eu/)

## Purpose
 
At 21X, we envision a future where financial markets are revolutionized by the power of blockchain technology. To achieve this, **we are building a groundbreaking trading venue utilizing distributed ledger technology (DLT) on a public permissionless blockchain.** This innovative infrastructure will enable highly efficient, secure, and disintermediated trading of financial instruments.

## Location
We are a European FinTech located in **Frankfurt (DE), Lindau (DE), Vienna (AT) and Ruggell (FL)**, but also make use of the latest technologies to **offer a flexible remote-working culture**. We think of ourselves as pioneers shaping the future of capital markets through blockchain technology and digital assets. Our strength is in bringing together a global team of capital market, digital asset and public blockchain experts with a proven track record in building regulated digital asset exchanges.
