# Platformen für die Betrachtung der Kursentwicklung

### [CoinCap](https://coincap.io/)
A service listing the market capitalization and exchange rates of hundreds of crypto-currencies, including bitcoin.

### [Bitcoin Average](https://bitcoinaverage.com/)
BitcoinAverage is the blockchain’s premier source of historical bitcoin price data featuring over 172 different fiat currencies and over 1440 cryptocurrency markets. We aggregate real-time bitcoin prices based on order books and transactions on over 50 major bitcoin exchanges around the world. 

### [Chicago Mercantile Exchange Bitcoin Reference Rate](https://www.cmegroup.com/markets/cryptocurrencies.html#products)
A reference rate that can be used for institutional and contractual reference, provided as part of investment data feeds by the CME.
