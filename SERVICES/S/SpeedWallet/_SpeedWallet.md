## the Claim
Speed Wallet is an **effortless Bitcoin Lightning-Wallet** for sending and receiving fast BTC payments running on most platforms.  

Send Bitcoin to split bills, and shared activities. Pay daily necessities with a zap, without waiting for approvals. Send a gift to your family and pay from any part of the world with Speed Wallet.

You can operate over any on-chain or lightning network, pay through the payment links, or set up one QR. 

Share your wallet address, QR code, or the LN address link, and get paid from anywhere, anytime.
