# Bech32 Address
An on-chain native SegWit Bitcoin address, also called a **Bech32 address**, is an advanced Bitcoin address format that **starts with "bc1". 

These addresses offer lower transaction fees, faster confirmations, and better error detection compared to older address types. They are ideal for optimizing transaction costs and can be used for sending and receiving Bitcoin, although it's essential to ensure the receiving wallet or exchange supports Bech32 addresses.
